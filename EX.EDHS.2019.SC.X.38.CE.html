Analyze the difference between science and pseudoscience.

While science itself is objective, scientists are not immune to bias. Social norms, political pressure, and funding sources can affect a scientist’s objectivity. One big example of this occurred in the mid-twentieth century. Scientists were paid by the tobacco industry to create studies downplaying the danger and addictive nature of tobacco.
